Overview

An application designed for task management and bug tracking (Bug Tracer). It enables teams to assign tasks, track their progress, and collaborate through comments for updates or additional details. With features like task assignment, progress monitoring, and real-time communication, the application ensures better organization and efficiency in handling tasks and resolving issues within a team. Its an essential tool for streamlining workflows and improving productivity in collaborative environments.

Challenge

Ensuring that only authorized users can access, modify, or perform specific actions within the application is crucial for security and functionality. Managing user roles and permissions requires a robust system to control who can do what and on which resources (tasks, bugs, comments, etc.)

Solution

Create a robust role-based access control (RBAC) system. Clearly define role permissions in your database (e.g., who can assign tasks, edit them, or close bugs).
